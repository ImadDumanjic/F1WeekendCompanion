# F1WeekendCompanion

F1 Weekend Companion is a modern, gamified social platform designed as a the ultimate "second screen" for Formula 1 race weekends. Built for Gen Z fans who want more than just watching — they want to compete, predict, and engage.

Features:
🏁 Race Dashboard — Countdown timer, next race info, and track stats
🎯 Predictions — Pick your podium, fastest lap, and safety car count
📊 Leaderboard — Compete with friends and climb the rankings
⚡ Live Race View — Real-time race progress and position tracking
🤖 AI Summary — Post-race analysis with highlights and movers
📅 Schedule & Weather — Full weekend timetable with live weather data
